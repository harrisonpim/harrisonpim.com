Notable Projects
Bookworm
2017

Most novels are, in some way, a description of a social network. Bookworm ingests novels, builds a solid version of their implicit character network and spits out a intuitively understandable and deeply analysable graph.
I presented a load of this work at PyData NYC 17 and Databeers LDN
Invisible Insights from TripAdvisor
2016-2017

Deep learning with an enormous corpus of text (using keras, gensim) to explore, classify, and quantify reviews of the British Museum. Negotiated collaboration with TripAdvisor, the Oxford Internet Institute, and The Alan Turing Institute. Presented at MCNxLondon and Oxford University.
